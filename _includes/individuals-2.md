**Sometimes we feel stuck in patterns that repeat in our relationships or within ourselves.**

You might notice things like:

- difficulty expressing your needs
- feeling overwhelmed in conflict
- shame or self-criticism
- confusion about intimacy or closeness
- feeling misunderstood in important relationships

Counselling offers a space where we can slow down and explore what is happening with curiosity and compassion.

Together we can begin to understand the deeper needs and emotions beneath these experiences.
