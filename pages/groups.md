Some things are easier to learn together.

In my workshops and groups, we explore how we relate to ourselves and others — especially in situations that feel challenging or emotionally charged.

These spaces are both practical and reflective, offering opportunities to learn, practise and experience new ways of communicating.

## Themes I work with

- communication and listening
- navigating conflict
- emotional dynamics
- shame and self-worth
- honesty and boundaries in relationships

## What to expect

- a structured and guided learning space
- opportunities to practise with others
- time for reflection and integration
- an emphasis on real-life situations

My approach is grounded in Nonviolent Communication and shaped by my experience working with individuals and groups.
